# Downloads

# How to batch download chapters?

Long press on a specific chapter to enter the multi-selection mode. Select the chapters you want to download, then click the download button.

# How to delete downloaded chapters?

Long press on a specific chapter to enter the multi-selection mode. Select the chapters you want to delete, then click the delete button.

# How do I download multiple chapters or series at the same time?

The app does not support parallel downloads from a single source to prevent potential IP bans due to excessive requests. While this might impact speed, it's preferable over rendering a source inaccessible.

Note that Tachimanga will download from up to three different sources in parallel.

# Is background downloading supported?

Not supported now. It is difficult to support background downloading due to Apple's background management strategy.

Starting from version 1.9, when you enter the download interface, the screen will automatically stay on, and you can stay on this page to wait for the download to complete.

# Why does the download sometimes fail?

Chapters that can be read online are generally downloadable. You can click on the chapter to see if you can read them normally.
