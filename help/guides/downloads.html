# Downloads

# Is background downloading supported?

TIP

We are currently testing the background download feature in the TestFlight version. How to get TestFlight access for Tachimanga? (opens new window)

Not supported now. It is difficult to support background downloading due to Apple's background management strategy.

# Why does the download sometimes fail?

Chapters that can be read online are generally downloadable. You can click on the chapter to see if you can read them normally.

# What determines the download speed?

Several factors affect the speed of a download:

  1. Sources: Download speed primarily depends on the source. If the source's servers are under heavy load or have limited bandwidth, it can slow down your download speed. You can try different sources and choose the one with the best service quality.

  2. Your bandwidth: Your internet connection's bandwidth directly impacts download speed. Higher bandwidth typically means faster downloads.

  3. Number of download threads: The number of simultaneous threads can affect speed. More threads can increase speed up to a limit, based on your internet connection and the server's capabilities.

  4. Connection quality: The link quality between your network and the source's server can influence speed. Using an appropriate VPN can sometimes improve the connection quality.

# How to batch download chapters?

Long press on a specific chapter to enter the multi-selection mode. Select the chapters you want to download, then click the download button.

# How to export downloaded chapters?

Not supported.

# How to delete downloaded chapters?

Long press on a specific chapter to enter the multi-selection mode. Select the chapters you want to delete, then click the delete button.
